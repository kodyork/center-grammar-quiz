センター試験 文法語法クイズ 2014

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全10問)

2014年度 (本試験)

問1

When I looked out of the window last night, I saw a cat [ 297 ] into my neighbor's yard.

答え: 2. sneaking
see + O + doing (現在分詞) で「Oが〜しているのを見る」。知覚動詞の構文。猫が忍び込んでいるところを見た。

問2

Ever since they first met at the sports festival, Pat and Pam [ 298 ] each other.

答え: 3. have been emailing
Ever since ... (〜以来ずっと) があるので、過去から現在までの継続を表す現在完了進行形が適切。

問3

My mother asked me [ 299 ] we should go out for lunch or eat at home.

答え: 3. whether
... or ... があることから、「〜すべきかどうか」を尋ねたとわかる。whether (if) ... or ... で「…かどうか」。

問4

My wife wanted to have our son [ 300 ] dinner for us, but I ordered a pizza instead.

答え: 1. cook
have + O (人) + 原形不定詞 で「Oに〜してもらう」。使役動詞の構文。「息子に料理してもらいたかった」。

問5

I took it for [ 301 ] that we were free to use the school gym on Saturday.

答え: 2. granted
take it for granted that ... で「…を当然のことと思う」。

問6

Could you [ 302 ] me who is planning Dan's birthday party?

答え: 4. tell
tell + 人 + 疑問詞節 で「人に〜を教える、伝える」。say to me ... や talk to me ... の語順は不可。

問7

We were shocked when the cashier added [ 303 ] the bill and the total was 20,000 yen.

答え: 4. up
add up ~ で「〜を合計する」。

問8

The ( A ) of treatment at the hospital is much lower for ( B ) who have health insurance. [ 304 ]

答え: 2
(A) 治療の「費用」は cost。(B) ... who have ... と関係代名詞節に修飾される代名詞は those (～な人々)。

問9

Even though I ( A ) spent two years in the US, I've never ( B ) to the Grand Canyon. Maybe I'll go next year. [ 305 ]

答え: 3
(A) 「かつて、一度」の意味の副詞 once。(B) have never been to ~ で「〜へ（一度も）行ったことがない」。

問10

My mother is trying very hard to ( A ) ends meet, ( B ) she never lets me buy anything unnecessary. [ 306 ]

答え: 4
(A) make ends meet で「収入内でやりくりする」。(B) 一生懸命やりくりしている「から（so）」、不必要なものは買わせてくれない。
