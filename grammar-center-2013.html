センター試験 文法語法クイズ 2013

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全10問)

2013年度 (本試験)

問1

I understand [ 287 ] of our students are working part-time in the evening to pay their school expenses.

答え: 4. most
most of ~ で「〜のほとんど」。almost は副詞なので almost all of ~ なら可。

問2

Of the seven people here now, one is from China, three are from the US, and [ 288 ] from France.

答え: 4. the others
特定の集団（7人）のうち、「1人」→「3人」→「残りの全員」を指すので the others を使う。7 - 1 - 3 = 3人なので複数形。

問3

My brother [ 289 ] have been very popular when he was a high school student. He still gets lots of New Year's cards from his former classmates.

答え: 1. must
must have done で「〜だったに違いない」。過去の事柄に対する強い肯定的な推量。今でも年賀状が来ることから判断している。

問4

Eric's friends, Minoru and Sachiko, will be here at seven this evening. He [ 290 ] doing his homework by then.

答え: 3. will have finished
by then (その時までには) という未来の完了時点を示す表現があるので、未来完了形 (will have done) を使う。「宿題を終えてしまっているだろう」。

問5

Our family doctor suggested that our son [ 291 ] a complete medical checkup every year.

答え: 1. get
suggest that S (should) + 原形...。提案・要求・命令などを表す動詞に続く that 節内では、動詞は原形（または should + 原形）になる（仮定法現在）。

問6

Japan [ 292 ] of four larger islands and many small islands.

答え: 1. consists
consist of ~ で「〜から成る、構成されている」。

問7

Did you have a chance to meet your grandfather [ 293 ] the winter vacation?

答え: 1. during
during + 特定の期間（名詞）で「〜の間に」。while は接続詞なので後ろに S+V が続く。

問8

I don't enjoy going to Tokyo. It's hard for me to put [ 294 ] all the crowds.

答え: 4. up with
put up with ~ で「〜を我慢する」。= tolerate。

問9

When my younger brother and I were children, my mother often asked me to keep [ 295 ] him so he wouldn't get lost.

答え: 1. an eye on
keep an eye on ~ で「〜から目を離さないでおく、〜を見守る」。

問10

I was offered a good position with a generous salary, but I decided to turn it [ 296 ] because I wanted to stay near my family.

答え: 2. down
turn ~ down で「〜（申し出など）を断る」。= refuse, reject。
